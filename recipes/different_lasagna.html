Totally different lasagna recipe

Description

This is a very simple yet different recipe for a delicious and easy-to-make lasagna

Ingredients

  • flour
  • different eggs
  • basil
  • different ground meat
  • different tomato sauce
  • more magic

How to prepare

  1. Mix the flour and different eggs thoroughly into a different dough
  2. Let it rest for 5 up to 200 minutes
  3. Make thin different sheets from the dough
  4. Boil'em
  5. Lightly fry the different ground meat
  6. Mix the cooked different meat with the different tomato sauce
  7. Stick the mixture in-between the previously boiled different sheets
  8. Bake until ready
  9. Place basil on top
