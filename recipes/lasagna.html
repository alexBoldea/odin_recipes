Lasagna recipe

Description

This is a very simple recipe for a delicious and easy-to-make lasagna

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • basil
  • ground meat
  • tomato sauce
  • magic

How to prepare

  1. Mix the flour and eggs thoroughly into a dough
  2. Let it rest for 5 up to 200 minutes
  3. Make thin sheets from the dough
  4. Boil'em
  5. Lightly fry the ground meat
  6. Mix the cooked meat with the tomato sauce
  7. Stick the mixture in-between the previously boiled sheets
  8. Bake until ready
  9. Place basil on top
